Chamonix Area: 12.07 - 16.07 (4 nights)

Complete 5-Day Chamonix Itinerary from Saint-Gervais-les-Bains
Perfect family plan (2 adults + teens 13/16/17) based in Saint-Gervais (20-min drive to Chamonix). All trails 6-9 km medium difficulty, nature-focused. Drive Aosta -> Saint-Gervais: 95 km via Mont Blanc Tunnel (55 EUR roundtrip).

Day 6 (July 12): Arrival Day - Chamonix Stroll + Lac des Gaillands
9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Drive Aosta -> Chamonix (95 km, 1.5 hrs).
11:30 AM - 2:00 PM: Chamonix Village (P+R Flegere/Savoy 10 EUR + 5-min bus):
Arve River walk, Mont Blanc fountain photos, alpine church
Lunch: La Caleche creperie (galettes 12-18 EUR/person)
2:30 - 3:30 PM: Lac des Gaillands (15-min drive): 3 km flat lakeside path + beach
4:00 - 4:45 PM: Drive to Saint-Gervais apartment check-in
Evening: Dinner Le MonBlanc brasserie (15-20 EUR/person)
Hiking: 3 km easy. Pure arrival relaxation.

Day 7 (July 13): Lac Blanc Loop via L'Index -> Cheserys Lakes (7.5 km)
Drive: Saint-Gervais -> Flegere/INDEX base Les Praz (25 min, P+R 10 EUR)
08:30 AM: Flegere gondola -> L'Index chairlift (35 EUR/adult RT, 18 EUR/teens = 129 EUR total)
UP (2.5 km, 1 hr): L'Index (2,590m) -> gravel path -> Combe des Crochues -> Lac Blanc (2,352m)
10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Lunch Refuge Lac Blanc terrace (soup/croutes 15-20 EUR) + lake photos
LOOP RETURN (5 km, 3 hrs):
Lac Blanc back door -> "Lacs des Cheserys" wooden steps
Chalets des Cheserys (small lakes, marmots)
2 metal ladders over slabs -> Col des Cheserys junction
RIGHT "Flegere 1h30" -> Tete aux Vents meadows -> Flegere station
3:30 PM: Gondola down -> Saint-Gervais
Gear: Hiking boots, poles, 2L water/person.

Day 8 (July 14): Grand Balcon Sud - Flegere -> Brevent Summit (8.5 km)
Drive: Saint-Gervais -> Flegere gondola base (25 min)
9:00 AM: Flegere gondola up (30 EUR/adult RT)
9:45 AM - 2:00 PM: Grand Balcon Sud south-facing balcony:
Follow "Grand Balcon Sud -> Brevent" red signs from Flegere station
Larch forests -> alpine meadows -> Brevent mid-station -> summit (2,525m)
Lunch: Brevent summit panoramic terrace (25 EUR/person)
2:30 PM: Brevent cable car down to Chamonix
3:00 - 5:00 PM: Chamonix village (free bus from valley)
Evening: Bus Saint-Gervais (30 min total)

Day 9 (July 15): Lake Passy + Gorges du Diosaz (Relaxed Teen Day)
9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Lac de Passy (15-min drive):
Beach volleyball, paddleboard rental (15 EUR/hr), 4 km lakeside loop
Karting du Mont Blanc (10-min heats, 90 EUR total)
Mont Blanc backdrop, ice cream at plage bar
1:00 - 4:00 PM: Gorges du Diosaz (10-min drive):
Suspended metal walkways over 50m gorge + waterfalls
Vertigo bridges, via ferrata intro (teens love thrill)
2 km loop (8 EUR/adult, 5 EUR/teens = 26 EUR total)
Evening: Chamonix street art walk + Fenetre Sports (scooter rentals)
Hiking: 6 km easy/thrilling. Perfect teen entertainment.

Day 10 (July 16): Cirque du Fer-a-Cheval (Epic Finale)
8:00 AM - 5:00 PM: Drive Saint-Gervais -> Sixt-Fer-a-Cheval (45 min)
Cirque du Fer-a-Cheval trail (8 km loop, 400m up, 3-4 hrs):
Europe's largest glacial cirque, 500m waterfalls cascade from cliffs
Chamois spotting, Gorges du Fer viewpoints
Lunch: Refuge du Fer-a-Cheval or picnic
Evening: Return Saint-Gervais (sleep)

Family Logistics
Total hiking: 35 km (7 km avg), all <=9 km, 300-600m elevation
Tickets: 500 EUR total (gondolas/chairlifts; teens 50% off)
Parking: Saint-Gervais hotels include; Chamonix P+R 10 EUR/day
Gear: Hiking boots, poles, swimwear (Passy), 2L water/day
Weather: July ideal; check mountain-forecast.com

Backup Attractions & Rainy Day Options

Alpine Museum (free, central)
Street Art + scooter rentals (fun, active)
Tramway du Mont Blanc (175 EUR total)
Parc de Merlet (deer park, 4 km trails if light rain)
Piscine de Saint-Gervais (indoor pool, 30 EUR total, top choice)
