Lake loop + chapel photos (Matterhorn towers above)

Back by 18:00 (sunset ~21:00 in July)

Safety Buffers Built-In
Plateau Rosa snow: Guaranteed year-round glacier skiing (26 km slopes maintained)
Lift closing: Upper sections typically run until 16:30-17:00; your 14:15 Schwarzsee departure = 2+ hr cushion
Conservative timing: Each lift segment includes queue/wait buffers
Weather escape: If clouds hit, you're already descending from mid-mountain

Tickets (2 adults + 3 teens 13/16/17)
"Breuil -> Schwarzsee via Plateau Rosa" roundtrip: ~140 EUR/adult, 70-90 EUR/teens = 450-500 EUR total
Buy at Cervinia base or online cervinia.it. Passports for Testa Grigia border check.
Total day: 600 EUR incl. parking/lunch/snow rentals. Perfect family pacing with kids' snow time + iconic Matterhorn views at 3 levels!
https://anywhereweroam.com/matterhorn-alpine-crossing/comment-page-1/

Day 3 Updated: Lilaz Falls + Valnontey Meadows & Cogne Dining
Perfect relaxed finale in Gran Paradiso from Argnod (45-min drive to Cogne). Free parking, easy trails for family.
Schedule:
8:30 AM - Drive to Lilaz Falls Parking: Search "Cascate di Lillaz parking" (GPS: 45.602 deg N, 7.453 deg E).
9:30 AM-12:00 PM - Lilaz Waterfall Trail: 3 km RT through forest to 20m cascade + natural pools (swimmable in July heat). Ibex spotting likely.
12:30 PM - Drive 10 min to Valnontey Meadows (2 km past Cogne center): Search "Valnontey, 11012 Cogne AO" (GPS: 45.579 deg N, 7.352 deg E).

How to Reach Valnontey Meadows:
From Lilaz parking, drive through Cogne village center (follow brown "Gran Paradiso Park" signs), continue straight 2 km up narrow but paved road along Urtier torrent. Pass first hamlets, arrive at flowery Valnontey basin ringed by Gran Paradiso glaciers. Park at main lot near Paradisia Alpine Garden entrance (free in summer). Wide pastures perfect for picnics; short flat paths to torrent viewpoints with marmots/chamois.

3:00-5:00 PM - Explore:
Stroll flower meadows (July bloom peak)
Visit Paradisia Garden (12 EUR/adult, 7 EUR/teens; 1,000+ alpine plants)
Torrent-side trails (spot golden eagles)

Cogne Dining Recommendation: Lou Ressignon (top family choice):
Search "Lou Ressignon Cogne" (GPS: 45.613 deg N, 7.355 deg E; Localita Valdotain 63).
Traditional Valdostan: Seupa a la vapelenentse soup, polenta cugna, Fontina fondue
Teens love carbonara + house gelato (15-25 EUR/person)
Outdoor terrace, park views; open till 10 PM. Reserve: +39 0165 74902.
6:00 PM - Return to Argnod.
